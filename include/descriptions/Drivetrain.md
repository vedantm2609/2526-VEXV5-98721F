The Drivetrain class is responsible for the movement of the robot's drivetrain. The .h and .cpp contain all of the functions that are used to control the base of the robot. Specifications include:

- 4 Omniwheel (mutli-directional) 3.25 inch (diameter wheels)
- 4 Traction (encapsulated between omniwheel) 3.25 inch diameter wheels
- 4 VEX 11W Smart Motors with blue motor cartridges (600 rpm). 
- 2 VEX 5.5W Smart Motors at 200rpm. 

The Drivetrain Class functions these modes driving styles that allow the driver to have different drive modes such as Arcade Drive, Curvature Drive, and Tank Drive. 

No deadzone is implemented per driver preference, allowing for maximum sensitivity and control.
